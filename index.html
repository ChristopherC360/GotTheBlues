Meet Blue

Blue is a curious creature. This curiosity has led him into places he probably doesn't belong. Help Blue escape this place.

There are many pitfalls that Blue may fall through. To combat this, blue has the special touch. Each time he steps on a real block, that block will turn red.

The Ghosts

These little guys can smell death a mile away. Each death the blue incurs has the potential to summon a Ghost. Do try to be careful. They may look cute, but one touch could kill.

Doors

Doors are your friends... Sometimes... They can be your salvation, or your damnation at the same time. Pressing the down arrow on them will allow you to go through them and see where they lead.

Jumpers

Jumpers are fun, until you jump and land somewhere you shouldn't have. They are your way of getting to places you wouldn't normally be able to reach. Just jump on them and going BOING!

Controls

These are your basic controls. Left and Right will move you from side to side. Up will let you jump, space will also accomplish this. And finally, Down will let you go through doors.
